# Ratio Budget

A minimalist, high-performance personal finance tool designed to help you master the **50-30-20** and **65-20-15** budgeting rules.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Instant Analysis**: Get a clear breakdown of your spending vs. ideal ratios in real-time.
- **Budgeting Modes**: Toggle between 50-30-20 (Basic) and 65-20-15 (Conservative) modes instantly.
- **Efficiency Score**: A proprietary metric that measures how well your spending aligns with your chosen strategy.
- **Privacy First**: All data is stored locally in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.
- **Rich Analytics**: Bar charts for allocation comparison and donut charts for spending distribution.
- **Offline Capable**: Works without an internet connection once loaded.

## üìà Budgeting Ratios

### 50-30-20 Rule
- **50% Needs**: Housing, utilities, groceries, insurance.
- **30% Wants**: Dining out, hobbies, entertainment.
- **20% Savings**: Emergency fund, investments, debt repayment.

### 65-20-15 Rule
- **65% Needs**: Essential expenses.
- **20% Wants**: Lifestyle spending.
- **15% Savings**: Consistent saving.
